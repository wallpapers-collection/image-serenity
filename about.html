About Image Serenity

Image Serenity is a lightweight gallery that curates anime and photo imagery from a static dataset. It focuses on quick browsing, search, and a calm visual experience.

What You Can Do

  • Browse curated lists with pagination or infinite load on mobile.
  • Search by title, description, author, category, or keywords.
  • Open detail pages for full metadata and original image links.

Tech Stack

Pure HTML/CSS/JS with a JSON source (`raw/datas.json`), no backend required.
